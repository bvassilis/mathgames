MATH BRAWL 1v1

Defeat opponents by solving Math Problems!

  • Jump (Up/W) and hit the correct answer orb with your racket (Space).
  • Press Up/W in mid-air to Double Jump! Press S/Down to bend down and dodge.
  • Hitting the wrong orb triggers a LIGHTNING STRIKE penalty!
  • The enemy will shoot slow tennis balls at you. Dodge them!
  • If time runs out, the game freezes!
  • Choose right while frozen to block the Lightning with a shield, choose wrong to take full damage!

Desktop: A/D to move, Up/W to jump, Space to attack, S/Down to bend.
Mobile: Use on-screen buttons.
